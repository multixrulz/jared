Passion Driven

Today a remarkable thing happened to me. Something that’s never happened before – one of those things that you read about, but never quite believe.

I found a book in my letterbox! – in fact, the same book was in all the letterboxes in our building, and in a number of other apartment buildings too. It’s called “Passion Driven” and its appearance made me think of who was responsible. Who had the finance and the enthusiasm to give a motivational book to hundreds of strangers? I don’t know, but it sure made my day.
