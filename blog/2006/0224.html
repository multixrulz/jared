Idiot!

A long time ago, dealing with Thai and Karen people who spoke English as a second (or third? or fourth?) language, that English has a remarkably large number of words for “people of lesser intelligence”. Do we in the English-speaking world have an overabundance of stupidity, or an obsession with it? Anyway, now I’m going to try to list them all… (perhaps a useful language lesson?)

  • nouns
    • idiot
    • moron
    • dolt
    • imbecile
    • twit
    • dimwit (and various other *wits, mostly not spoken in polite company)
    • dope
    • dufus
    • dill
    • bonehead
    • lame-brain
    • imbecile
    • pinhead
    • numbskull
    • fruitloop
    • fruitcake
    • loonytune
  • adjectives
    • stupid
    • dumb
    • dopey
    • moronic
    • docile (for some reason we used to use this all the time, although it means something else quite different)
    • idiotic
    • dim
    • dull
    • silly
    • brain-dead
