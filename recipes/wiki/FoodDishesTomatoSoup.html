Tomato Soup

Tried this on Jan 8, 2020

  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 onion chopped or sliced thin
  • 4 cloves garlic, diced, minced, or crushed.

Fry it up. Then add the flavours.

  • 1 teaspoon Oregano
  • Bit of salt
  • 1 teaspoon thyme

Now add a big (800g) tin of diced tomato, maybe tomato paste, maybe water...

Sources for future reference 

Recipe 1

2 tablespoons olive oil
2 onions, diced coarsely
6 garlic cloves, coarsely diced
1 tablespoon oregano
2 bay leaves
3 sprigs fresh thyme or 1 tablespoon dried thyme
1 tablespoon dried basil

1 pinch salt and black pepper, to taste
1 pinch sugar, to taste
2 (400g) tins tomatoes
1-2 tablespoons tomato paste, optional
500ml chicken stock
150ml cream or milk, to taste

Recipe 2

2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
1 onion, chopped
4 cloves garlic, minced
4 tomatoes, chopped
3 ½ cups cherry tomatoes, halved
¾ cup vegetable broth
2 bay leaves
2 sprigs fresh basil, divided 

Recipe 3

1 tablespoon unsalted butter or margarine
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 onion, thinly sliced
2 large garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
2 (28 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
1 cup water
1 tablespoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt, plus more to taste
freshly ground black pepper to taste
1 pinch red pepper flakes
¼ teaspoon celery seed
¼ teaspoon dried oregano
