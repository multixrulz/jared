Pe Baya Kyaw

  • 1 cup dried yellow split peas (soak overnight and pat dry)
  • 1 medium yellow onion, chopped finely
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander
  • 1 red chilli, minced
  • 1.5 teaspoons salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 2 teaspoons minced ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • oil
  1. Put all the ingredients in a food processor and whizz up
  2. Make patties
  3. Refrigerate at least 3 hours
  4. Fry
  5. Eat with sauce (or use as a falafel, etc).
