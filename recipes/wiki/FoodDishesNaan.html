Naan

makes 5 naans.

Put 1 teaspoon yeast and 1 teaspoon sugar into 0.25 cup warm water. Let it do its thing (about 10 minutes)

Mix 2 cups flour, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon oil and 0.25 cup yoghurt in a big bowl. (Can use milk instead of yoghurt)

Add the yeast mixture little by little and knead. Add water as necessary.

Knead to a very soft dough. It'll be sticky at first, but stops sticking later. This takes 5-10 minutes.

Let it rise double (1-2 hours)

Make lemon-size balls. Roll it out with a rolling pin or by hand. Fry in a dry pan on one side, brush with ghee (or margarine) on the other while cooking, then flip.

Notes

5/1/2020 - I put in quite a bit extra water and then it was really wet and sticky, so a lot of flour went on during kneading.
