Pizza

For two pizzas.

Make our own pizza base:

Mix up

  • 4 teaspoons yeast
  • 2 tablespoons sifted plain flour
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 cups warm water

Let the yeast do its thing until it's frothy.

Sift in

  • 5 cups plain flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 tablespoons oil (4x20mL = about 1/3 of a cup)

Knead it until it's elastic. At the same time, clean and oil two bowls. Put the dough into the bowls and let it rise (double size)

Oil the workbench and sprinkle semolina. Knead the dough lightly for 1 minute.

Spray the tray and roll out the dough and put it on the tray.

Top the pizza and bake - can start cooler (180?) and heat up as it shows signs of being cooked through. Grill at the end to cook the cheese if necessary.
