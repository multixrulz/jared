Chicken Korma

Marinade - mix it all together

  • 0.5kg chicken
  • 3 tablespoon natural yoghurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Blend together all this:

  • 1 cup sliced onion
  • 1.5 tablespoon oil
  • 2-3 cardamom seeds
  • 4 almonds
  • 6 cashews
  • 1/4 cup natural yoghurt

Cooking it whatever

  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 cloves
  • 1 cardamom
  • 2 inch cinnamon

Then:

  • Saute the marinated chicken (5-7 minutes)
  • Add the paste, 1/2 cup water and 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Add 1/2-3/4 teaspoon garam masala, 1.5teaspoon coriander powder
  • Add fresh coriander
