Vegetable Korma

about 3 servings

Blend together:

  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 25mm ginger
  • 1 big chilli
  • 0.25 cup raw cashews

Mix up:

  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 0.5 teaspoons cinnamon
  • 0.5 teaspoons coriander
  • 0.5 teaspoons salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 0.125 teaspoons ground cardamom

Heat up 1 tablespoon oil and add the paste and a little later add the spice mix. Cook for a couple of minutes.

Add 0.5 cups coconut milk, 0.75 cups natural yoghurt, 1.5 teaspoons brown sugar and a selection of vegetables coming to about 3 cups. The first time we used 2 smallish potatoes, diced, 0.5 cups frozen snow peas, and 1 cup diced zucchini. Simmer until cooked.

Add 2 small tomatoes cut into crescents. Don't add earlier or the starchy foods will not cook.

Serve with basmati rice and Naan
