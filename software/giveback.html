Give Something Back

Free Software is awesome! There’s a wonderfully wide and deep range of software you can get “for free”, and which respects your freedoms to share, study and improve it.

But we shouldn’t just be takers.

If you appreciate Free Software, give something back to the wider Free Software community. You can do this in many ways, for example:

  • Contribute to a Free Software project. Some examples:
    • Programming
    • Testing
    • Writing documentation
    • Promoting it
  • Write some Free Software of your own.
  • Send money to the people who write Free Software.

That’s right, if you have no other way to give something back, you could send money. For most software, this is the only way you have to give back – with Free Software, you get to choose.
